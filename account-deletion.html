How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your Kribb account and all associated data using either of the two methods below. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

1
Open the Kribb app on your phone Make sure you are signed in to the account you want to delete.
2
Go to Profile tab → Settings Tap the Profile icon at the bottom right of the screen, then tap Settings.
3
Scroll down and tap "Delete Account" A confirmation prompt will appear. Confirm to permanently delete your account.
4
Deletion is immediate Your account and all associated data are deleted instantly. You will be signed out automatically.

Request Deletion by Email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by sending an email to our support address. Include the email address associated with your Kribb account.

We will process your request and confirm deletion within 7 business days.

Request Deletion via Email
Use the email address registered to your Kribb account when sending this request so we can identify and delete the correct account.
